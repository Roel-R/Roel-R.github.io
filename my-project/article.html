10 Most influential Games of All Time

When video games are made on its core it was shaped based on some of the most poplar games in the past.

The games on the list has helped shape the games industry throuhout the years

  1. Everquest
    • The first major Massively Multiplayer Online Role Playing Game(MMORPG).
  2. Street Fighter II
    • First Modern Fighting game.
  3. Doom
    • Pioneered the First Person Shooter (FPS) games.
  4. Grand Theft Auto
    • Gave players the Freedon in violence.
  5. Final Fantasy
    • Brought the Japanese Role Playing Games (JRPG) to mainstream.
  6. Pokemon
    • Started the Catch-and-battle system in games
  7. Legend of Zelda
    • Popularized dungeon exploration and open worlds
  8. Warcraft Franchise
    • Helped pave way to other genres of games
      • Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA)
      • Tower Defense Games
      • World of Warcraft - The most popular MMORPG game of all time
  9. Minecraft
    • Created the open world survival genre
  10. Mario
    • Created the Platformers Genre
